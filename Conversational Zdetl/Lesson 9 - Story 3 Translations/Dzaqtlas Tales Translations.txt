As the sun sets in the western desert, a lone wanderer begins her long trek across the wastes.
Pliebrimaniriemvra feva tlachakache`, zartals fronzhivrnar tlatzens chiala vrendamenad.


Ahead lies the valley of the Standiev`ts, ravaged by plague and ruled by barbaric warlords.
Tiech iqe standievtschani, zilchatokpivra fronzhe dazej, iazh qlomens baza iakotzatl. 


They are the kind of men who always rise to power when civilization dies. Brutal, undisciplined killers. Monsters. Bad.
Shtiefrifraqens, zhinqetseadlam michteqikriem dra fliedonqesiad. Shtoma, kazhatsqlie krazojnad. Baz.
